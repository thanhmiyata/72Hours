GOT QUESTIONS?

Find answers to your most pressing questions about our emergency kits.

Why 72 hours?

FEMA and Canada both recommend being prepared for at least 72 hours. This kit is built to cover that critical period.

Shelf-life of the food
and water?

All our Food and water have up to a 5-year shelf life (delivered within 1-6 months after the manufacturing date).
The food & water will need to be replenished after the expiration date.
We have our own food & water refill kits, so it saves you the convenience of having to find the items yourselves!

Can the kit be
customized to meet
specific needs or
requirements?

Yes, our emergency kits can be customized to meet your specific needs.
Please contact us directly, and we'll work with you to create a personalized kit tailored to your requirements.

Are the food rations
gluten-free?

The standard food ration bars in our Emergency Kits are all nut-free and vegetarian friendly but are not gluten free.
If you have a gluten allergy, please reach out to us and we'll happily work with you to make sure you've got a solution.

Do you do custom
Emergency Kit orders
for large groups?

Yes, we do accept custom orders for large groups.
For inquiries regarding custom orders, please email us directly at orders@72hours.ca.
We'll be happy to discuss your specific requirements and provide you with a tailored solution to meet the needs of your group.
