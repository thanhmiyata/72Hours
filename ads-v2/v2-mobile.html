Awakening
Day 1: The Wake-Up Call
I never considered myself the “prepper” type. I always thought emergency kits were for doomsday people, not regular families like mine. But last winter, the power went out in our neighborhood for two full days.
No heat, no lights, no hot meals.

My kids huddled under blankets, scared, and I felt helpless.
That night, I realized something painful: I wasn’t READY. If something bigger happened, like a wildfire, flood, or earthquake, we wouldn’t last more than a few hours.

Day 2: The Search for Peace of Mind
I started Googling “how to prepare for an emergency,” and everything I read said the same thing:
